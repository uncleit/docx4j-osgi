Ecma formal publications

Two types of formal publications are prepared by Ecma:

  • Ecma Standards - see index or list
  • Ecma Technical Reports - see index or list

The Ecma historical archive which contains the scanned copies of all withdrawn Ecma Standards is available here.

A Standard or a Technical Report is a formal document prepared by an Ecma Technical Committee and approved by the Ecma General Assembly. A majority of at least two-thirds of all the ordinary members is required for approval.

Ecma International often submits its Standards to ISO/IEC JTC 1's fast track approval procedure which adheres to the WTO TBT guidelines.

Ecma Standards and Technical Reports are made available to all interested persons or organizations, free of charge and copyright, in printed form and, as files in Acrobat ® PDF format.

How to obtain Ecma publications


WARNINGS

The liability and responsibility for the implementation of an Ecma Standard rests with the implementor, and not with Ecma.

However, implementors might want to consult the Ecma "Code of conduct in patent matters".

Only the printed version or the PDF file can be considered an official version of the formal document.
